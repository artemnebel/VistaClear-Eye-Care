Meet Dr. Lee

Dr. John H. Lee

O.D. — Doctor of Optometry
PA NJ DE
Board Certified
Outstanding Achievement in Primary Eye Care

Dr. John H. Lee earned his undergraduate degree from Boston University in Boston, MA, and his Doctorate of Optometry from The Pennsylvania College of Optometry. His clinical education was completed at The Eye Institute of Philadelphia — one of the nation's leading eye care institutions.

Dr. Lee received specialty training at the William Feinbloom Low Vision Center and the Thomas P. Lynch Pediatric Clinic in Philadelphia, giving him deep expertise in caring for patients with low vision and children with complex vision needs. He is board certified in Pennsylvania, New Jersey, and Delaware, and has been recognized for outstanding achievement in primary eye care services.

Patient Safety

Office Safety Protocols

We take your health seriously. Our office follows enhanced safety measures for all appointments.

Hand Sanitizer

Available throughout the office for patients and staff.

By Appointment Only

All visits are scheduled in advance to limit waiting room crowding.

Arrive 15 Minutes Early

Please plan to arrive 15 minutes before your scheduled appointment time.

Staff Protective Equipment

Clinical staff wear appropriate protective equipment during all examinations.

Cancellation Policy

48-hour advance notice required. A $50 no-show fee applies to missed appointments without notice.

Limited Guests

To maintain a safe environment, please limit accompanying guests unless necessary.
