The New Patient Health History Form is required for all new patients. It collects your health history, current health status, and allows you to communicate questions, concerns, and goals regarding your eye health and vision.

What to Bring

  • Completed New Patient Health History Form
  • Vision insurance card and photo ID
  • Current eyeglasses or contact lenses
  • List of current medications
  • Previous eye exam records (if available)
  • Payment method (insurance co-pay, FSA card, or cash/credit)

Cancellation & No-Show Policy

We understand that schedules change. If you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, please give us at least 48 hours advance notice.

A $50 no-show fee will be charged for missed appointments without adequate prior notice. This helps us serve all our patients effectively. To cancel, please call 215-935-6908.
