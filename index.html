About Our Practice

Expert Care,
Personal Touch

Dr. John H. Lee trained at Boston University and earned his Doctorate of Optometry from The Pennsylvania College of Optometry. With specialty training at the William Feinbloom Low Vision Center and Thomas P. Lynch Pediatric Clinic, he is uniquely qualified across a wide spectrum of eye care.

We dedicate ourselves to providing the highest quality eye care in a comfortable, unhurried fashion. Well-informed patients are active participants in their own eye health — and we take the time to ensure you always leave with answers.
